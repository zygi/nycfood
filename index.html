how to build your search

Your search will allow you visualize how the type of food (e.g. Chinese) relates to other information (e.g. neighborhood). All you have to do is choose the cuisine type and the additional information you want to compare!

STEP 1

Choose one cuisine type. The distribution of restaurants that fall under this category will be shown on the map in red.

STEP 2

Choose one extra dataset. This dataset will be shown in the map in blue. This extra information will allow you to see what cuisine type is related to.

How to read this map

  1. The cuisine type you chose is used to filter NYC restaurants.
  2. The filtered restaurants are shown on the map in red.
    The darker the red, the more restaurants there are in the area.
  3. The extra dataset you chose is overlaid in blue on top of the restaurant data.
    For some datasets like Income and Property Value, the darker the blue, the higher the value. For other datasets like Subway Stops, the information is shown as point data.
